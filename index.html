How I work

A simple, structured way to get from question to decision.

1 · Brief

We define your key questions, constraints, timeline and outputs (e.g. deck, model, report).

2 · Research & Modelling

I run structured desk research, stakeholder calls (if needed) and build the first models or frameworks.

3 · Synthesis

We review intermediate results, refine assumptions and focus on what really changes the decision.

4 · Handover

You receive final deliverables (slides, Excel, brief) plus a short walkthrough so your team can work with it independently.
