Urban Air Mobility Simulation (AMIUS) — TUM Chair of Traffic Engineering
Research assistant · Aimsun Next · U-space / UAM integration
+

Contributed to AMIUS, a collaborative initiative exploring integrated U-Space and the integration of drone/VTOL UAM services into multimodal traffic simulation in Bavaria.

My work

  • Simulated integration of VTOL and drone-based UAM services using Aimsun Next.
  • Modelled vertiport networks, air corridors and intermodal hubs as scenario inputs.
  • Analysed KPIs: delay time, harmonic speed, traffic flow and RGap% across scenarios.

Outcome

  • Scenario comparison insights supporting infrastructure planning and policy recommendations.
